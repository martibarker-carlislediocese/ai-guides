Church House — AI Guide

🎓 Education

Supporting schools, governors, and the wider Diocese with faster document work, clearer communications, and better-organised information.

The Education team supports a large number of Church of England schools across the Diocese — dealing with inspection preparation, governor support, statutory guidance, and communication with headteachers, governing bodies, and the Diocese leadership. The volume of document work is significant, and much of it follows consistent patterns that AI handles well.

Gemini is the everyday tool for drafting and summarising. Claude + Cowork adds the ability to work with your Outlook inbox and SharePoint files — pulling together correspondence from multiple schools, maintaining resource libraries, and generating documents directly from your existing data without manual copy-pasting.

Gemini — school documentation and communications

✨ Gemini

Open in your browser · No setup · All staff

Help schools prepare for inspection

Schools facing an Ofsted or SIAMS inspection need to produce self-evaluation documents, governor statements, and supporting evidence packs. Paste draft content into Gemini and ask it to improve clarity, structure, and professional tone — or ask it to write a first draft from bullet points you provide.

Try this prompt "I'm writing a school self-evaluation summary for a SIAMS inspection. Here are the key points we want to make about our Christian vision and pupil wellbeing. Write these as a coherent 3-paragraph narrative in a tone appropriate for school inspection."

Summarise inspection reports

Paste an Ofsted or SIAMS report into Gemini and ask it to summarise the key findings, judgements, and areas for development — saving hours of reading and distillation when you need to brief governors or the Director of Education quickly.

⏱ A 40-page inspection report summarised to a 1-page briefing in 2 minutes.

Write governor newsletters and updates

Draft a brief to Gemini on what you want to communicate and it will produce a polished governor newsletter or circular — covering statutory changes, training opportunities, or diocesan news in a format appropriate for a governor audience.

Explain statutory guidance changes in plain English

When DfE or the Church of England publishes new guidance, paste the relevant sections into Gemini and ask it to explain what has changed and what schools need to do differently. Much faster than reading dense government documents from scratch.

Create curriculum policy templates

Ask Gemini to draft subject policy templates for schools — RE, PSHE, collective worship — that can be adapted to each school's context. Consistent starting points that reduce workload for headteachers and subject leads.

🖥️ Claude + Cowork

Opens your files · Works with Outlook and SharePoint · Things Gemini can't do

Pull together correspondence from multiple schools

When you need an overview of what's been communicated with a specific school — or across all schools in a cluster — Claude can search your Outlook inbox, gather the relevant email threads, and produce a chronological summary of what's been discussed and what's outstanding.

Example workflow "Search my Outlook inbox for all emails from St Mary's Primary School. Summarise the key topics discussed in the last 3 months and flag any items that appear to need a response."
⏱ Reviewing months of school correspondence: 2 hours → 10 minutes.

Maintain a schools resource library on SharePoint

Claude can help you organise and maintain a SharePoint resource library for schools — renaming files consistently, moving documents into the right folders, and generating a structured index of what's available and where. Schools and governors can then find what they need without having to ask.

Generate personalised letters to multiple schools

If you need to write to every school in the Diocese about a policy change, a training opportunity, or an inspection update, Claude can generate personalised versions of a standard letter — inserting the correct headteacher name, school name, and any school-specific details — from a list on SharePoint.

⏱ 40 personalised letters: a day's work → 30 minutes.

Prepare governor briefing packs from SharePoint documents

Point Claude at a folder of documents on SharePoint — policies, reports, minutes, financial summaries — and ask it to produce a consolidated briefing pack for an upcoming governor meeting. Claude reads each document and synthesises the key points into a single, structured briefing.

🔒 Data safety for Education

Fine to share with AI tools

  • Inspection report text (publicly available)
  • Draft policy documents and templates
  • Governor newsletters and communications
  • Statutory guidance documents
  • General school correspondence (non-sensitive)
  • Resource library content and training materials

Do not share with any AI tool

  • Individual pupil data, names, or records
  • SEND or safeguarding information about children
  • Staff HR or disciplinary information
  • Confidential governor or leadership discussions
  • Complaints or investigation details
  • Any data protected under GDPR that identifies a child
