Church House — AI Guide

📋 Project Management

From project briefs and status reports to Power BI dashboards and team-enabling templates — AI as your project infrastructure partner.

Project Management at Church House does two things: it delivers its own projects, and it helps other teams work better. AI can support both. For your own projects, it handles the documentation cycle — briefs, agendas, minutes, status reports, risk registers. For other teams, it can help you build the SharePoint structures, Power BI dashboards, and reusable templates that improve how the whole Diocese operates.

Gemini is excellent for producing project documents quickly. Claude + Cowork is where the real capability sits — connecting to your Outlook, SharePoint, and Excel data to automate the reporting cycle and build infrastructure other teams will actually use.

Gemini — project documentation at speed

✨ Gemini

Open in your browser · No setup · All staff

Write project briefs and charters

Describe a project to Gemini and it will produce a structured project brief — objectives, scope, stakeholders, timeline outline, dependencies, and success criteria. A proper first draft in 5 minutes.

Try this prompt "Write a project brief for implementing a new HR system at the Diocese of Carlisle. The project involves [key steps]. Key stakeholders are HR, Finance, and IT. Timeline is approximately [period]."

Build RACI matrices and responsibility grids

List the project activities and the people involved, and Gemini will produce a RACI matrix — clarifying who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed for each element.

Draft stakeholder communications plans

Describe your project and its stakeholder groups, and Gemini will produce a communications plan — what to communicate, to whom, how often, and through which channel. Adaptable to any project size.

Write meeting agendas and action log templates

Gemini can produce consistent, well-structured meeting agendas and pre-formatted action log templates — ready to drop into SharePoint for each project.

Create risk registers

Describe your project and Gemini will generate a risk register with likely risks, probability and impact ratings, and suggested mitigating actions — a strong starting point that you refine with your knowledge of the specific context.

🖥️ Claude + Cowork

Opens files · Works with Outlook and SharePoint · Builds dashboards

Generate status reports from your email threads

Claude can read a project's Outlook email thread, identify what's been agreed, what's in progress, what's overdue, and who's responsible — then draft a formatted project status report without you synthesising it manually.

Example workflow "Search my Outlook inbox for emails about the [project name] project. Summarise progress against the key milestones, flag any items that appear stuck, and draft a one-page status report for the project board."
⏱ Status report compilation: 2 hours → 20 minutes.

Minutes to action tracker, automatically

Share meeting minutes from SharePoint and Claude will extract every action point, assign it to the right owner, suggest a due date based on context, and draft individual follow-up emails — all from one instruction.

Build SharePoint project tracking structures

Claude can help you design and build SharePoint lists that serve as your project tracker — with the right columns, views, and metadata — and write the documentation that explains to other teams how to use them. It can also help you set up consistent folder structures across multiple projects.

⏱ SharePoint list setup and documentation: days → hours.

Create Power BI reports and DAX queries

If your project data lives in SharePoint or Excel, Claude can help you design Power BI reports — suggesting the right visualisations, writing DAX measures for calculated fields, and producing documentation that explains what each report shows. Describe what you want to see and Claude writes the query logic.

Example workflow "I have a SharePoint list tracking project milestones with columns for: project name, milestone, due date, status, and owner. Write DAX measures to calculate % complete by project, and suggest a Power BI dashboard layout to show portfolio status."

🔗 Enabling other teams — the multiplier effect

Project Management's most significant AI opportunity isn't just improving your own team's output — it's building infrastructure that other Church House teams can use. Claude + Cowork can help you create standardised SharePoint structures for Finance, HR, and Safeguarding; build Power BI dashboards that pull from their data; and develop reusable templates that reduce how much every team has to reinvent from scratch.

For example: the Safeguarding team struggles with SharePoint organisation. Project Management, working with Claude, could build them a clean, intuitive folder structure with consistent naming conventions — something that would take days manually but can be designed and documented in hours with AI support.

Similarly, a Diocese-wide project portfolio dashboard in Power BI — showing every active project, its status, and its owner — could be built by Project Management using Claude to write the DAX and design the report, then shared across Church House so leadership always has a live view of what's in flight.
