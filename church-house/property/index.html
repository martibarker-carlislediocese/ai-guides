Church House — AI Guide

🏗️ Property

Managing a large portfolio of buildings, contractors, and inspection cycles — AI as your correspondence engine, record organiser, and document processor.

The Property team manages one of the Diocese's most complex portfolios — churches, vicarages, offices, and other buildings across Cumbria, each with its own maintenance history, inspection schedule, and contractor relationships. The correspondence and documentation load is significant: surveys, specifications, planning applications, contractor instructions, and statutory compliance records.

Gemini handles the drafting — contractor letters, survey response notes, specification summaries. Claude + Cowork goes further: it can pull together all correspondence with a specific contractor from Outlook, read survey documents from SharePoint, and help maintain the master property records that underpin the team's work.

Gemini — property documents and correspondence

✨ Gemini

Open in your browser · No setup · All staff

Draft contractor instructions and correspondence

Describe the work required and Gemini will write a professional instruction letter to a contractor — clearly setting out the scope, timeline, access arrangements, and any special requirements. Consistent professional tone without starting from scratch each time.

Try this prompt "Write a formal instruction letter to a roofing contractor for emergency repair works to the nave roof at [church name]. Works required: [description]. Access via [details]. Works to be completed by [date]. Diocese of Carlisle is the instructing client."

Summarise survey and inspection reports

Quinquennial inspections and building condition surveys can run to 50 or more pages. Paste the report into Gemini and ask it to summarise the urgent items, category 1 and 2 defects, and recommended actions — a one-page briefing that non-technical readers can act on.

⏱ A 60-page quinquennial report summarised in under 3 minutes.

Write planning application supporting statements

Planning applications for listed buildings require carefully worded supporting statements — explaining the heritage context, the proposed works, and why they are appropriate. Gemini can produce a first draft that your team and any appointed architect then refine.

Draft responses to quinquennial inspection recommendations

When an architect issues quinquennial recommendations, the PCC and diocese need to respond. Paste the recommendations into Gemini and ask it to draft a structured response — acknowledging each item, indicating the proposed approach, and noting any constraints.

Create maintenance schedules and asset registers

Ask Gemini to produce a planned maintenance schedule template — with recommended inspection intervals, responsible parties, and typical costs — for a property type such as a Victorian stone church or a 1970s vicarage.

🖥️ Claude + Cowork

Opens your files · Works with Outlook and SharePoint · Things Gemini can't do

Pull together all correspondence with a contractor

When a dispute arises, or when you need to review the history of a project, Claude can search your Outlook inbox for all emails with a specific contractor, organise them chronologically, and produce a timeline of what was instructed, what was delivered, and what remains outstanding — without you scrolling through months of emails.

Example workflow "Search my Outlook inbox for all emails to and from [contractor name]. Organise them by date and produce a chronological summary of the project history — what was agreed, what was done, and any outstanding items or disputes."
⏱ Rebuilding a project correspondence history: half a day → 20 minutes.

Maintain your property register on SharePoint

Claude can help build and maintain a master property register in SharePoint — with columns for property type, inspection date, next inspection due, responsible officer, and outstanding works. It can read the existing records, flag overdue inspections, and draft reminder emails to relevant PCCs.

⏱ Identifying all properties with overdue quinquennials: hours → minutes.

Process multiple survey reports in bulk

If you receive several quinquennial or condition survey reports in a single season, Claude can read each one from SharePoint and produce a comparative summary — ranking buildings by urgency, identifying common defect types, and giving you an overview of the maintenance liability across the portfolio.

Generate inspection reminder letters automatically

From your property register on SharePoint, Claude can identify which buildings have a quinquennial inspection due in the next 12 months and generate reminder letters to each PCC — personalised with the church name, last inspection date, and the appointed architect's details — ready for your review and sending via Outlook.

Organise and index historic property documents

SharePoint folders that have accumulated years of documents — surveys, correspondence, specifications, certificates — can be brought into order. Claude can review the folder, rename files consistently, and create a structured index document so the team can find what they need without searching manually.
