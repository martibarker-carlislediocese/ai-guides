Church House — AI Guide

👥 HR & Governance

From job adverts and minutes to Subject Access Requests — practical AI tools that cut administrative overhead and keep the Diocese organised.

HR and Governance teams deal with high volumes of structured but time-consuming work — drafting job adverts, taking minutes, organising committee papers, and responding to data requests. Much of this follows patterns that AI handles well. The key is knowing which tasks are safe to delegate and which need human oversight.

Gemini handles everyday drafting and document work in your browser. Claude + Cowork goes further — it can read files from SharePoint, process Outlook email threads, and help organise complex document sets without you copying and pasting anything.

Gemini — everyday HR drafting

✨ Gemini

Open in your browser · No setup · All staff

Write job adverts and job descriptions

Paste in the key responsibilities and requirements and Gemini will produce a well-structured, professional job advert — in plain English, with consistent formatting. It can write for different audiences: formal for diocesan roles, more accessible for parish-facing positions.

Try this prompt "Write a job advert for a [role title] at the Diocese of Carlisle. The role involves [key responsibilities]. We're looking for someone with [requirements]. The salary is [range]. Use an approachable but professional tone."

Create interview question banks

Describe the role and Gemini will generate a structured set of interview questions — including competency-based questions, scenario questions, and questions that probe values alignment. A useful starting point that you can refine.

Draft induction materials and handbooks

Give Gemini a list of topics to cover and it will produce a structured draft of induction materials — welcome sections, key policies summary, who's who, and what new starters need to know in their first week.

Summarise HR policies in plain English

Paste in a formal policy document and ask Gemini to write a plain-English summary for staff — turning dense policy language into something readable and practical.

Try this prompt "Summarise this disciplinary policy in plain English for a member of staff who has never read an HR policy before. Focus on what they actually need to know."

Draft appraisal templates and forms

Gemini can produce structured appraisal templates, objective-setting frameworks, and self-evaluation forms — tailored to Diocese roles and values.

🖥️ Claude + Cowork

Opens your files · Works with Outlook and SharePoint · Things Gemini can't do

Minutes to action tracker — automatically

Drop a set of meeting minutes from SharePoint and Claude will extract every action point, identify who is responsible, assign a suggested deadline, and format everything as a table — then draft individual follow-up emails to each action owner.

Example workflow "Open the March Finance Committee minutes from SharePoint. Extract all action points, list who is responsible for each, and draft a follow-up email to each person with their specific actions."
⏱ 45 minutes of post-meeting admin becomes a 5-minute review task.

Organise committee and board papers

Claude can read a folder of synod or committee papers from SharePoint, summarise each document, and produce a single briefing note — so decision-makers arrive at meetings prepared rather than overwhelmed by the volume of papers.

Build and maintain an organisation structure

Point Claude at a SharePoint list or spreadsheet of staff roles and reporting lines and it will help you structure, update, and export the information — ready for an org chart tool or for inclusion in board papers.

Process recruitment applications from Outlook

When applications arrive in Outlook, Claude can read them, extract key information (name, experience, qualifications), compile a comparison table, and flag which candidates meet the essential criteria — before you've opened a single email manually.

⏱ Longlisting 20 applications: 2 hours manually, 15 minutes with Claude.

Draft contract and offer letter templates

Working from a master template on SharePoint, Claude can populate personalised offer letters or contracts for each new appointment — substituting the correct role title, salary, start date, and terms — ready for your review and signature.

📂 Subject Access Requests — a proven Diocese use case

The Diocese IT team has already used Claude + Cowork to successfully process a Subject Access Request (SAR) — one of the most time-consuming HR obligations. What would typically take days of manual document review and redaction was completed in a fraction of the time.

Here's what Claude can do with a SAR: when you receive a SAR, you need to collect all relevant documents, redact information about third parties, organise everything chronologically, and produce a packaged response. Claude can read document folders from SharePoint, identify which files contain the data subject's information, redact third-party names and details, and organise the output into a structured response pack.

This is not a theoretical use case — it has been done successfully within the Diocese. Contact Marti Barker (IT Support Manager) if your team receives a SAR and you'd like support running this process.

🔒 Data safety for HR & Governance

Fine to share with AI tools

  • Job advert and job description drafts
  • Interview question templates
  • Policy documents and handbook drafts
  • Anonymised meeting minutes and action logs
  • Committee paper summaries (non-confidential)
  • Induction materials and general HR templates

Do not share with any AI tool

  • Individual disciplinary or grievance case details
  • Specific named staff performance information
  • Medical or occupational health information
  • Salary or payroll details for named individuals
  • Unredacted SAR documents (use Claude + Cowork for redaction — don't paste into Gemini)
  • DBS or criminal record information
